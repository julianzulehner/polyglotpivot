Welcome to Polyglotpivot

Polyglotpivot is a free vocabulary trainer specifically designed to help you track and fill gaps in your vocabulary, especially if you speak multiple languages.

As a private project, Polyglotpivot ensures all your data and vocabulary entries are stored locally and will never be used or published. However, it is always good practice to only share information you are comfortable making public.

To get started, simply register an account and dive right in. Your feedback and ideas for improvement are always welcome. Feel free to post in the home section of the website (visible once logged in) or send an email to polyglotpivot@gmail.com.
